# BorderLine: Landing Page Copy & Marketing Strategy Brief

This document defines the user-facing copy, value propositions, and landing page blocks for BorderLine, incorporating the empirical data from our survey (N=37) to drive conversion.

---

## 1. Landing Page Architecture & Copy Blocks

### Block 1: Hero Section (The Hook)
* **Goal**: Instant clarity on what BorderLine does and who it is for.
* **Recruiter-Facing Hero Headline**: 
  > **"Source verified builders from Africa's fastest-growing talent pipeline."**
* **Talent-Facing Hero Headline**:
  > **"Your work is your credential. Build a verified portfolio. Get hired globally."**
* **Sub-headline**:
  > *BorderLine is the trust layer between Africa's emerging builders and the companies that need them — turning raw capability into verified, hire-ready profiles with AI.*
* **Primary CTAs**:
  - **For Recruiters**: `Start Hiring`
  - **For Creators/Students**: `Create Your Profile`

---

## 2. Block 2: The Core Problem (Empirical Survey Data)
This section uses our survey statistics to validate the problem statement and hook the reader.

* **Section Title**: 
  > **"Africa has the talent. The world has the demand. The infrastructure doesn't exist yet."**
* **Graphic/Text Callouts (Survey Data)**: *These statistics from our N=37 survey must be presented on the landing page using dynamic, scroll-triggered flip-up wheels (odometer effect) to emphasize the magnitude of the problem:*
  * **70% of early-career builders are excluded by experience requirements they can't meet yet.**
  * **92% have no structured way to prove what they can build.**
  * **59% face daily connectivity barriers — the platform must meet them where they are.**

---

## 3. Block 3: The BorderLine Solution (Feature Copy)

### Feature 1: The AI & Peer-Verified Portfolio Builder
* **Headline**: **"AI-audited & peer-verified portfolios that prove capability, not credentials."**
* **Description**: 
  - *No more generic text descriptions or empty claims. Paste your GitHub repo or upload your project notes. Our AI parsing engine analyzes your files, tags your technical skills, and structures your work into a professional case study. Once published, your peers and fellow platform developers review and endorse your project, securing a combined trust seal that employers can rely on.*

### Feature 2: Low-Data WhatsApp Assistant
* **Headline**: **"Stay connected to opportunity — even on 2G."**
* **Description**:
  - *Data costs shouldn't hold you back. Send a lightweight text message to our WhatsApp assistant: 'I just finished a Python API project.' Our AI worker updates your live web portfolio in the background, matching you with new micro-tasks and jobs instantly.*

---

## 4. Block 4: Target Personas Value Props

### 👥 For Technologists (Students & Junior Freelancers)
* **Turn projects into proof**: AI + peer reviews convert your work into structured, validated case studies employers trust.
* **Proactive AI Applications**: Let our AI agent match and apply to relevant micro-gigs on your behalf in real-time, meaning you never miss an opportunity.
* **Access without infrastructure**: Update your profile, receive matches, and get paid via WhatsApp and Mobile Money.
* **Earn from day one**: Connect with micro-gigs, startup contracts, and global remote roles.

### 🏢 For Recruiters & Startups
* **Verified capability, zero vetting overhead**: AI-structured and peer-vouched case studies replace unformatted repos and text resumes.
* **Hire cross-border, compliantly**: Localized payments, contract templates, and compliance built in.
* **Tap a pipeline traditional platforms miss**: 70% of Africa's digital workforce has zero presence on legacy hiring platforms.
