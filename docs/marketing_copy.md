# BorderLine: Landing Page Copy & Marketing Strategy Brief

This document defines the user-facing copy, value propositions, and landing page blocks for BorderLine, incorporating the empirical data from our survey (N=37) to drive conversion.

---

## 1. Landing Page Architecture & Copy Blocks

### Block 1: Hero Section (The Hook)
* **Goal**: Instant clarity on what BorderLine does and who it is for.
* **Recruiter-Facing Hero Headline**: 
  > **"Hire Vetted African Tech Talent on Practical Proof-of-Work, Not Resumes."**
* **Talent-Facing Hero Headline**:
  > **"Bypass the Resume Trap. Turn Your Code into Verified Portfolios Instantly."**
* **Sub-headline**:
  > *BorderLine uses AI to convert your raw class projects, side hustles, and Github repositories into professional, result-oriented case studies. Share verified proof of what you can build—directly to regional startups and global employers.*
* **Primary CTAs**:
  - **For Recruiters**: `Source Vetted Talent`
  - **For Creators/Students**: `Build Your AI Portfolio (Free)`

---

## 2. Block 2: The Core Problem (Empirical Survey Data)
This section uses our survey statistics to validate the problem statement and hook the reader.

* **Section Title**: 
  > **"Why the Traditional Job Pipeline is Broken for African Tech Talent"**
* **Graphic/Text Callouts**:
  * **70.3% of Junior Talent is Locked Out**: *Traditional entry-level roles gatekeep talent by demanding 3+ years of professional history, creating an impossible "chicken-and-egg" cycle.*
  * **91.9% of Resumes Fail to Showcase Skills**: *Nearly all students and junior freelancers rely on standard text resumes or unorganized links that fail to represent what they can actually build.*
  * **The Internet Barrier is Real**: *Over 59% of creators face frequent network disruptions due to local data tariffs, isolating them from heavy cloud portfolios and international remote job search dashboards.*

---

## 3. Block 3: The BorderLine Solution (Feature Copy)

### Feature 1: The AI Portfolio Builder
* **Headline**: **"From Raw Repositories to Enterprise-Ready Case Studies in Seconds."**
* **Description**: 
  - *No more generic text descriptions. Paste your GitHub repo or upload your project notes. Our AI parsing engine analyzes your files, tags your verified technical skills, and structures your code into a professional case study highlighting role responsibilities and technical impact.*

### Feature 2: Low-Data WhatsApp Assistant
* **Headline**: **"Keep Your Portfolio Updated with a Simple Text."**
* **Description**:
  - *Data costs shouldn't hold you back. Send a lightweight text message to our WhatsApp assistant: 'I just finished a Python API project.' Our AI worker updates your live web portfolio in the background, matching you with new micro-tasks and jobs instantly.*

---

## 4. Block 4: Target Personas Value Props

### 👥 For Technologists (Students & Junior Freelancers)
* **Build Trust Fast**: Get an AI-verified trust stamp on your portfolio projects, showing global employers you have the skills they need.
* **Low-Data Access**: Manage your applications and update your work history entirely via text commands on WhatsApp.
* **Fair Compensation**: Connect to micro-tasks and local startup contracts backed by secure escrow and paid out directly through **Mobile Money (MoMo)**.

### 🏢 For Recruiters & Startups
* **Zero Vetting Friction**: Stop reading text-heavy resumes. Read verified, structured case studies showing functional code capability.
* **Cross-Border Compliance**: Let BorderLine handle international contractor compliance, contract templates, and localized cross-border payments.
* **Ungated Talent Pipelines**: Gain direct access to hungry, pre-vetted entry-level creators from West and East African tech hubs.
